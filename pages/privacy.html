Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 12, 2026

At JERMAN, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our LindAV application and services.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us:

  • Account information (email, name, password)
  • Device information for security scanning
  • App usage data to improve our services
  • Payment information (processed securely through third parties)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide real-time threat protection
  • Improve and personalize our services
  • Send security alerts and updates
  • Process transactions and send billing information
  • Respond to customer support requests

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All data transmissions are encrypted using TLS 1.3, and we never sell your personal information to third parties.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. Contact us at privacy@jerman.com for any privacy-related requests.
