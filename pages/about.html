About JERMAN

Founded in 2020, JERMAN is on a mission to make enterprise-grade mobile security accessible to everyone. We believe that protecting your digital life shouldn't be complicated or expensive.

Our flagship product, LindAV, uses cutting-edge AI and machine learning to detect and neutralize threats in real-time, protecting over 50 million devices worldwide.

Our Mission

To provide world-class security solutions that protect individuals and businesses from evolving digital threats, while respecting user privacy and maintaining device performance.
